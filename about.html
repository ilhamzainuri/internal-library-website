About Zoid Rent

Your Reliable Outdoor Gear Rental Partner

Who We Are

Zoid Rent is an outdoor gear rental business based in Tlogo Agung 3 A, Malang City. We specialize in providing high-quality equipment for outdoor activities including camping, hiking, and mountaineering.

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by a group of nature enthusiasts, Zoid Rent started from a small garage in Tlogo Agung. The founders were passionate about outdoor adventures but found it difficult to access affordable, high-quality gear.

Driven by the motto "Rent Easily, Adventure Freely", Zoid Rent has grown to become a trusted name in outdoor gear rental, serving hundreds of adventurers across East Java.

Our Vision

To bring people closer to nature by making outdoor equipment accessible, affordable, and reliable.
